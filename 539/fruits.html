Fruits

These fruits are grown in the Philippines.

Banana- Not as sweet as the bananas found in the states.

Chico- Looks like a Kiwi on the exterior with a sweet flavor.

Coconut- It contains coconut water in the interior and has white, meaty texture that can be eaten alone or paired with other sweet treats.

Dragonfruit- Magenta in hue, with a firm texture. It’s sweet with tiny seeds in the interior.

Jackfruit- It’s kind of like a sweeter pineapple without the tartness.

Kamais- It is a tart fruit. It has a bit of a crunch in its exterior.

Mango- It’s texture can be similar to a peach but quite a bit softer with a nice sweetness.

Papaya- when it is ripe, its flavor reminds me a bit of a melon and mango combined.

Rambutan- best described from a website called Mother Nature Network (MNN) as a “sweet and sour grape.”
